Welcome New Interns!

Welcome to my webpage! The objective of this websit is to provide basic knowledge of the Supply Chain and Logistics industry and entail what day-to-day work looks like for new interns. If you ever get stuck or need a refresher during your workdays, this website has everything you need.
I think the term “Supply Chain” is incredibly vague and this website will be able to bridge that gap to entail what daily work looks like and how the company functions.

This will not only expand your knowledge of the industry I currently work for, but also I am hoping to use this website to show new hires and interns to help them learn as well. I am choosing to create an educational website because the intern orientation process of the current company I work for could definitely use some help, and no better opportunity to help out with that than this class and website. To have a website interns can always reference if they run into trouble is very valuable to have in the company.
